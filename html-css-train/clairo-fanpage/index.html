Claire Cotrill

20-year-old Claire Cottrill, better known as Clairo, is coming out in her own way. With unprecedented openness, her new music is making a statement about self-discovery, personal evolution, and the fluidity of sexuality. In 2017, the singer-songwriter went viral on YouTube with “Pretty Girl,” a self-produced music video made in her bedroom that’s since racked up over 32 million views. With her ep Diary 001 and honest lyrics in songs like “Flaming Hot Cheetos” and “4EVER”, she’s continued to inspire young people to embrace who they are. She’s toured with Dua Lipa, recently released Throwaway in collaboration with SG Lewis, and just announced a forthcoming tour with Khalid and a very personal record. Last May, the artist came out as bisexual to her fans. Ahead of her new album IMMUNITY, out August 2, them. sat down with the rising songwriter to hear about her new music, how she’s exploring her identity in her work, and how she’s using her platform for good.
